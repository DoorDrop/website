Frequently Asked Questions

What is DoorDrop? +
DoorDrop is an Australian platform that connects businesses needing letterbox delivery with verified local walkers. Every delivery is GPS-tracked and reported in real time.
How much does it cost? +
Pricing starts from $0.12 per door depending on volume. You set your budget and rate per door when creating a campaign. There are no hidden fees.
How do I become a walker? +
Sign up on the app, complete your walker profile with your suburb, availability, and verification documents (police check, WWCC, ABN are optional but recommended), and start browsing available campaigns in your area.
How do I know my flyers were delivered? +
Every delivery is recorded with GPS coordinates on a live map. You can see exactly which doors were delivered to, when, and by whom. Campaign dashboards show real-time progress.
Which areas do you cover? +
DoorDrop operates across Australian suburbs. Coverage depends on walker availability in your area. Major metro areas like Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Perth have the most walkers.
